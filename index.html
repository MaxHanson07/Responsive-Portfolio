About Me

My name is Max Hanson and I am from Mount Vernon, WA. I am a current college student working towards a degree in computer science. If I could choose one word to describe how I want to live my life, I would want my life to have "variety." I love jobs and tasks that challenge me to think and allow me to approach problems in a variety of ways. This is what draws me to coding. My skill set is multifaceted. I take pride in combining my diverse set of skills in a unique way to get a job done, rather than specializing in one specific skill. This approach helps me avoid the dread of monotony while allowing me to explore all of my interests.

In my free time I like to enjoy spending time in nature, whether it be hiking in the mountians, jet skiing on the river, or playing golf with family and friends.
